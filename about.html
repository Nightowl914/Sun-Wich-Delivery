Are you hungry after a long class but you still have a long day to go? Don't fret. We are the solution for short breaks between class and saviour of students. Just order online and we will deliver it to you if its within campus range.

We are in collaboration with Bite and Taste, the famous sandwich cafe in Sunway Campus which is the top choice for students to get their snacks and food to recharge themselves.

Bite & Taste will create the delicacies and we are responsible to deliver it to you for as cheapest as possible. So that you can save your energy and your precious break time
